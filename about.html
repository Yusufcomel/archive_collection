Preserving Heritage, Stitch by Stitch

Nusantara Attire is a digital archiving initiative that honors and protects the rich cultural history of traditional Malay apparel, such as Kain Songket, Baju Kurung, and Baju Kebaya. The purpose of this platform is to record and communicate the tales behind these clothes so that people in the future can learn about and appreciate their historical and artistic importance.

We believe that traditional clothing is more than simply fabric; it's art, identity, and history.

Our Vision

To be a leading digital reference for traditional Malay attire, promoting cultural pride and safeguarding the legacy of Nusantara textiles and fashion

Our Mission

  • To digitally archive and showcase the beauty and diversity of traditional Malay clothing.
  • To educate the public about the historical and cultural significance of kain songket, baju kurung, and baju kebaya.
  • To support the continuation of traditional craftsmanship through awareness and appreciation.
  • To provide accessible and well-researched resources for students, researchers, and cultural enthusiasts.

Target Audience

Our target audience includes:

  • Students and researchers in cultural studies, fashion, and history.
  • Heritage preservationists and museum curators.
  • Designers and textile artists seeking inspiration from traditional styles.
  • The general public, especially the younger generation, who want to reconnect with their roots.
